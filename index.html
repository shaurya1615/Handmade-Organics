About Us

My son always suffered from severe eczema. I had tried all the medical and home remedies but he would still be left scratching. As a reluctant entrepreneur but budding chemist and biological investigator, I set out to understand dry skin and how we can find solutions within nature and after 5 years of foraging for better, more nourishing raw ingredients was born Handmade Organics.

I am a mathematician by profession so my knack for numbers and passion for organics helped build the organic skincare products of HANDMADE ORGANICS.

We have sampled our products across various skin colours, types and interests. We felt if our products speak to the customer so should the people who made the products. The consumer is the stakeholder in every business he buys into. So we resolved to not just create skincare that is good for your skin but good for the people who farm these great raw ingredients. Our goal is to create no nonsense skincare for sensitive, dry skin from fairtrade naturals.

We understand that all dry skin is different. My son is 29 and I am 52. Its taken us this long to understand the miracles of our body and adapt our learning accordingly.
